Save your daughter!

Your archnemesis Brutus Iskariot has escaped from prison and has kidnapped your daughter, you must save her at all costs. You can progress through the game by clicking on the buttons, if no buttons are present you must click on the text to proceed the story. Selecting the wrong choice will result in your death. You will have the choice of returning to the previous choices or start over again. There are 5 endings to the story.
